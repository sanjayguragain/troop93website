Established 1922

A Century of
Fullerton Heritage.

For over 100 years, Troop 93 has been a cornerstone of the Fullerton community. Founded in 1922, we are one of the oldest scouting organizations in Southern California, dedicated to fostering growth, adventure, and leadership.

What started as a small group of ambitious local youth has evolved into a thriving community of scouts who carry forward the traditions of excellence, service, and environmental stewardship that defined our founders.

Legacy Point

Our Core Mission

The mission of Scouting is to prepare young people to make ethical and moral choices over their lifetimes by instilling in them the values of the Scout Oath and Law.
