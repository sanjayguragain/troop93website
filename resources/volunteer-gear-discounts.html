1

The Application

Select Non-Profit, Volunteer, or Outdoor Professional. If "Scouts/BSA" is not listed, select "Other".

  • Organization: Boy Scouts of America
  • Website: Scouting.org
2

The Justification

Copy and paste this text:

"I am a registered parent volunteer with Scouts BSA Troop 93, Fullerton. Our troop camps regularly throughout the year and participates in outdoor skills training and high adventure activities. The gear I am purchasing will be used frequently to support these scouting activities. Access to pro pricing helps our family invest in durable, high quality equipment while keeping scouting affordable."
3

Proof of Affiliation

Log in to My.Scouting.org.

Leaders: Account > My Profile > Registered Positions > Download Membership Card.

Parents: Account > My Profile > Personal Information > Print > Export to PDF.
