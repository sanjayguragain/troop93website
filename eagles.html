The Eagle Scout
Honor Roll

Since 1922, Troop 93 has been committed to developing young men of character. The rank of Eagle Scout represents the culmination of years of dedication, leadership, and service to the community.

A Century of Excellence

Becoming an Eagle Scout is a milestone that very few reach. It requires earning at least 21 merit badges, demonstrating Scout Spirit, and planning, developing, and leading a service project—the "Eagle Project"—that benefits the community.

Those listed here have navigated the rigorous process of the Eagle Board of Review and have committed themselves to the principles of the Scout Oath and Law for a lifetime. We are proud to honor their achievement.
